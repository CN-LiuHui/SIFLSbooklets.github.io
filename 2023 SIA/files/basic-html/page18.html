除学科领域外，学生还将钻研IB文凭课程的三大核心课程，即知识论、扩展性论文以及创
                   造力、行动力和服务项目。
                   Apart  from  the  mentioned  subject  areas,  students  will  delve  into  another  three  core  subjects  of  the  IB
                   Diploma, namely Theory of Knowledge, Extended Essay and Projects of Creativity, Activity and Service.











                                      知识论要求学生深入了解知识本身。这是一门哲学学科，旨在让学生提出问题，
                     知识论              深入了解知识的构成方式以及对个人和文化方面的知识偏见进行批判性思维。
                       TOK
                                      Theory  of  Knowledge  gets  students  to  look  deeply  into  knowledge  itself.  It  is  a
                                      philosophical  subject  designed  for  students  to  ask  questions,  look  deeply  at  how
                                      knowledge  is  constructed,  and  think  critically  about  biases  in  knowledge  both
                                      personally and culturally.




                                      拓展论文可以被视为微型论文，在此类论文中，学生可以探索其学科组内感兴趣
                    扩展论文              的领域。EE使学生能够进一步提高研究技能，并在与导师进行的一系列谈话中反
                       EE
                                      思自己的主题。
                                      The extended essay can be considered a mini-thesis in which the student explores an
                                      area of interest within their subject groups, or as an interdisciplinary inquiry across
                                      two study areas of global significance. This essay enables students to further their
                                      research skills as well as reflect on their topic during a series of interviews with a
                                      supervisor.


                                      专为IB文凭课程学生的个人发展而设计。通过一个自我指导的项目，学生必须在
                创造、行动、服务              与创意、体育活动和志愿活动交织的体验之旅中使用问题解决技能、沟通和决策
                       CAS
                                      能力。
                                      CAS is specifically designed for the personal development of IB Diploma students.
                                      Through  a  self-guided  project,  students  must  use  problem-solving  techniques,
                                      communication and decision-making skills in an experiential journey interweaving
                                      with creativity, physical activity, and volunteering.
