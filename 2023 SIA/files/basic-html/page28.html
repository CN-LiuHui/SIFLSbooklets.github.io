                       D               全球学术竞赛 Support for global competitions


                    升学辅导老师会根据学生专业申请的意向，给出匹配的全球学术竞赛建议。学生在校即可进行学术竞
                赛的营地补习，学科老师全力助力学生申请世界名校。希雅也是全球众多学术竞赛的考点，学生在校即可
                参 加 美 国 数 学 竞 赛 （A M C ） 、英 国 数 学 竞 赛 （B M O ） 、美 国 物 理 竞 赛 （B M O ） 、英 国 物 理 竞 赛
                （BPhO）、英国化学竞赛（UKChO）、加拿大化学竞赛（CCC）、澳大利亚生物竞赛（ASOB）。
                SIA counsellors will make suggestions for global academic competitions according to the intentions of the
                students  with  regard  to  the  major  they  are  applying  for.  Students  can  take  camp  tutoring  for  academic
                competitions at school, and subject teachers help students apply for world-renowned schools at every step.
                Our school is also the test centre for many academic competitions around the world. Students can participate
                in  the  American  Mathematics  Competition  (AMC),  the  British  Mathematical  Competition  (BMO),  the
                American Physics Competition (BMO), the British Physics Competition (BPhO), and the British Chemistry
                Competition (UKChO)., Canadian Chemistry Competition (CCC), Australian Biology Competition (ASOB).































                       E               冲刺顶级名校            Sprint for dream schools


                    对于申请英国牛剑等G5院校、美国藤校或其他世界顶尖名校的学生进行专属方案定制，通过学术成
                绩、国际竞赛、学术课题研究和论文、实习、推荐信、牛剑营等多维度完善学生申请方案。

                Exclusive program customization for students applying for the G5 super elite such as Oxbridge in the UK, and
                Ivy  League  schools  in  the  United  States  or  other  top  universities  in  the  world.  To  perfect  the  student
                application program through academic performance training, international competitions, academic research
                and papers, internships, letters of recommendation, and Oxbridge camps, etc.
